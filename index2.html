FIJI


Fiji is a beautiful place, where you can find amazing ingredients in unusual combinations! Join us for a sweet potato and banana salad and a cocunut milk curry.


Uzbekistan


Right through the silk road you'll find Uzbekistan, full with flavours and aroma spices. Let's try their traditional Plow tonight!

Grenada

Indonesia

Central African Republic


It's just humbling how much you can learn from a country's past and present through food. We were moved by CAR's Fufu and Kanda. Come with us for a taste of culture!


Japan


One of the most exciting countries of this project for sure, every dish requieres an attention to detail that is just... breath taking! We just couldn't do a day, we did a Japanese fest, so let's get going for some fluffly pancakes, tamari sushi, sweet potatos and shrimp tempura.


Luxemburg


Couldn't get any more central europe than Luxemburg, the perfect child of a German and a French! Comfort food is their thing, so if you are up for it, let's make Judd mat Gaardebounen and Verwurelter.


United States of America


What does such a huge and diverse country as the US have in common throught all of its states? Breakfast! Let's roll our sleeves up for cinnamon rolls, pancakes, scrambled eggs and crispy bacon!


Thailand


A gem in this crazy food world, Thailand just left us at awe with their millions flavours per bite. Don't trust us? Try with us the (for good reason) famous green curry and pad thai.
